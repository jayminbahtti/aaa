ABOUT US

For the past 11 years, Krushan Dham has served the real estate sector with zeal and dedication, constructing dependable homes that survive the test of time. Their tradition of expertise and devotion to anticipating market demands has given them a reputation as one of the industry's most trusted developers.

Krushan Dham has successfully supplied over 6,00,000 sqft of quality living space over 5 Projects buildings over the years, offering homes to over 500 happy families. The company's dedication to providing homes that inspire happiness and fulfillment is obvious in the 2500+ grins on the faces of pleased customers.

Krushan Dham has established itself as a trendsetter in the real estate sector by prioritizing quality, innovation, and customer happiness, setting new benchmarks of excellence, and elevating the expectations of discriminating homeowners everywhere.

MISSION & VISION

Timely delivery and Exceptional Quality

Krushan Dham Developers places a strong value on the timely delivery of high-quality houses. They have a talented team of architects, engineers, and builders who pay close attention to detail and employ premium materials and advanced construction processes to achieve both visual appeal and structural longevity. The company's track record of providing great homes on time demonstrates its unshakable dedication to quality and on-time delivery.

DIRECTOR’S DESK

Krushan Dham, founded in 2012 by Directors Kishorilal Sharma and Haresh Poorji, has had a significant effect in rapidly developing locations such as Usatane and Taloja.

The business distinguishes itself in the construction market by developing each home with passion and attention, anticipating the future demands of the next generation. Krushan Dham's dedication to completing projects on schedule and focusing solely on a single project has raised the bar for real estate innovation.
